Dear Hiring Manager,

Enterprise SAP environments are among the hardest surfaces to automate reliably — authentication layers, SAP GUI scripting quirks, cross-system data dependencies, and compliance requirements that change faster than test suites. I have spent years building automation against exactly this surface, covering GRC compliance, logistics goods-movement, and user-account integrity across production SAP systems.

Library-Level Engineering

I designed and built CreateXmlFileWaren.py — reads test data from Excel and generates SAP-structured XML for the Warenverkehr logistics workflow. I extended CommonListener.py to route results to Teams with KeePass-based credential retrieval — so webhook URLs never appear in source.

GRC Compliance Automation

I own the SAP GRC compliance suite: automated checks across PS1, PC1, and QV1 that flag unauthorized critical-role assignments before they become audit findings. A false negative has real audit implications — I treat the suite's accuracy as a production concern.

What I Bring

Technical depth at the library level combined with domain knowledge in SAP and GRC. I look for roles where test automation is treated as an engineering discipline — frameworks worth designing, tooling worth building, and suites worth owning over time.
